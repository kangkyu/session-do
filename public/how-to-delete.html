How to Delete Your Ongoings Account

A step-by-step guide to permanently remove your account and data

Account Deletion Guide

We understand that you may want to delete your Ongoings account. This guide walks you through the simple process to permanently remove your account and all associated data from our services.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all your data including your ongoing tasks, settings, and history will be permanently removed from our servers.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Log in to your Ongoings account

Open the Ongoings app and log in with your email address and password.

2

Navigate to your Profile

Tap on the profile icon or menu button (typically located in the top-right or bottom-right corner of the screen).

3

Access Account Settings

In the profile menu, look for "Account Settings" or "Settings" and tap on it.

4

Find the Delete Account Option

Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option. It's located next to the "Log Out" button.

5

Confirm Account Deletion

Tap on "Delete Account". You'll be taken to a confirmation screen that explains what happens when you delete your account.

After reviewing the information, tap on "Yes, Delete My Account" to proceed with the deletion.

6

Deletion Complete

After confirming, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted, and you'll be returned to the login screen with a confirmation message.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your Ongoings account, the following information is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account information (email address and authentication data)
  • All your ongoing tasks and responsibilities
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Usage history and activity logs
Note: Deletion is typically processed immediately, but it may take up to 48 hours for all your data to be completely removed from our backup systems.
